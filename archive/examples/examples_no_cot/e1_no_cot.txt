Claim: Stephen King wrote the novel that the movie directed by Stanley Kubrick that was sampled in the album \"Where Blood and Fire Bring Rest\" was based on.
Evidences: "Where Blood and Fire Bring Rest is the third full-length album released by metalcore band Zao on Solid State/Tooth & Nail. It was the first album to feature vocalist Dan Weyandt after the departure of Shawn Jonas along with new bassists/guitarists, Russ Cogdell and Brett Detar. The album contains a sample from the film \"The Shining\" at the beginning of \"To Think of You\", as well as a sample from the horror film \"The Prophecy\" during the intro to \"Ravage Ritual\".
